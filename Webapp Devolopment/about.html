About Us

Veggieworld supplies ready prepared vegetables to the catering and retail industry. We were established in 1999 and have since built a reputation for delivering a high quality customer service and for our wide variety of top quality produce. We have recently upgraded our factory to suit an ever changing and growing market. This has made us more efficient in meeting our customer demands.
